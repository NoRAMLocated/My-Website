Interviewing

Week 3 · Field Notes

From what I wrote and how I feel about the interviews, I can take away that interviewing is both difficult and interesting.

From my experience, I find that taking notes while the interviewee is speaking is incredibly difficult. My thought is that people who can talk and take notes can multitask at a superhuman level.

"It's frustrating that I have to make a recording of the interview, then go back and take notes on that."

I do love learning new things from people I get to interview — and it's a bonus if I know little about the subject.

Over the course of this project, I've learned that interviewing and taking notes each have their own challenges. The interviews I participated in were very informative and provided valuable insights for our final project.

Key Insight

Listening + note-taking = superhuman multitask

Workaround

Record first → review → then take notes

Bright Side

Love learning from unfamiliar subjects
