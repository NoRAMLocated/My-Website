The Chaos Inside My Computer

From what I wrote, I can assume that my view on organization is lower than it should be. I think that, from my experience with different organizational strategies, most do not align with how my brain functions.

I would like to use organizational strategies and have a schedule, but only a few of the tactics I have actually work. I usually feel frustrated because I have a hard time keeping track of things.

The most difficult part of my organizing struggle is keeping my files on my computer organized. My downloads are everywhere, I can't find my important documents, and if I want to find a photo, I'm better off looking online.

My best bet is creating tons of folders to store everything. I feel upset about these things, and I try more and more ways to help myself, but they rarely work.
