Empathy

On missed chances & what they could have taught me

A few years ago, I had an opportunity to volunteer at local community shelters for the homeless and at animal shelters. I chose not to take any of these opportunities. Thinking back, I don't remember why I decided not to.

Now, if I had, I believe it would have been a great, growing experience for me. It would have given me a deeper understanding of what empathy means and how I can help others.

Along with this, it could have shown me what real problems exist and how others are handling them — rather than just hearing what the media has to say about them.

— Noah Loar
